## Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks Identified

**Consumer Acceptance Risk (Medium)**: Extensive sampling, influencer partnerships, satisfaction guarantee, rigorous supplier selection for texture.

**Customer Acquisition Cost Risk (Medium)**: Multi-channel testing, focus on organic growth and referrals, optimize landing page conversion.

**Supply Chain Risk (Medium)**: Develop 2-3 backup suppliers, maintain 4-6 weeks safety stock, negotiate flexible MOQs.

**Competitive Response Risk (Low-Medium)**: Build strong brand identity quickly, focus on hard-to-replicate differentiation, establish retail relationships early.

**Price Sensitivity Risk (Low-Medium)**: Emphasize value proposition, offer subscription discounts, create smaller package sizes, position as health investment.
