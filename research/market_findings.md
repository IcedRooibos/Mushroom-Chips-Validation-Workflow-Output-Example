# Hong Kong Healthy Snacks Market Research Findings

## Market Overview

### Market Growth
- **CAGR 2020-2024**: 10.29% (strong demand for healthy snacks in Hong Kong)
- **Growth Rate 2023-2024**: 4.07% (suggesting sustained momentum)
- **Future Outlook**: Market poised for expansion and diversification

### Key Market Drivers
1. **Busy urban lifestyles** - consumers seeking portable, nutritious options
2. **Health and wellness awareness** - rising consumer prioritization of health
3. **Convenience demand** - on-the-go snacking for busy professionals
4. **Clean eating trend** - demand for natural and organic ingredients
